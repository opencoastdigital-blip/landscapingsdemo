Our Philosophy
01

Not just landscaping.
Land crafting.

Terranova was born from a simple conviction: the ground beneath your feet deserves the same creative ambition as the walls around you. We approach every acre, every garden bed, every stone edge as a design problem with a living, breathing answer.

Our team pairs horticultural science with spatial design thinking. The result isn't a lawn — it's a landscape that evolves, surprises, and endures across seasons.

What We Cultivate

From initial soil analysis to the final stone placement — every phase is designed, not just executed.

01

Landscape Design

Full-scope design from concept through planting plans. We map topography, sun paths, drainage, and sight lines to compose spaces that feel inevitable.

02

Hardscape & Masonry

Patios, retaining walls, fire pits, and walkways crafted from natural stone, reclaimed brick, and poured concrete. Structure that ages with grace.

03

Native Planting

Climate-tuned, regionally authentic plant palettes that support local ecology. Less irrigation, more life. Every bed is a micro-habitat.

04

Outdoor Living

Kitchens, pergolas, lighting systems, and entertainment areas engineered for year-round use. We extend your home into the ground it sits on.

05

Water Features

Ponds, streams, fountains, and rain gardens. Water adds motion, sound, and reflection — the sensory layer most landscapes are missing.

06

Seasonal Care

Pruning, mulching, fertilization, and seasonal rotations. We maintain what we build — because great landscapes are never finished, only tended.

How We Work

From Soil to Spectacle

01

Discover

We walk your land, study the soil, trace the light, and listen to what you imagine. Every detail informs the design.

02

Design

We produce detailed plans, 3D renderings, and material samples. You see the vision before a single shovel breaks ground.

03

Build

Our in-house crews execute with precision. Hardscape, grading, planting, irrigation — coordinated, on schedule, and clean.

04

Nurture

We stay on through the first growing seasons, adjusting, pruning, and ensuring everything roots deep and thrives long.
